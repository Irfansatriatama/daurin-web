GUIDES

Trash Drop Off
To access this service, you need to login/ register first. Make sure you have a Daurin account before requesting pick-up or other recycling services. Here are the steps:
  • 1. Before making a Drop Off request, make sure you have prepared the waste to be sold and the type of waste must be separated. Then pack in plastic or other containers.
  • 2. Open the Daurin application, select Start Recycling
  • 3. Next, choose the type of waste that you will sell.
  • 4. Set the approximate weight of your recyclables.
  • 5. Select the Drop Off service
  • 6. Specify the drop off address
  • 7. Select the delivery date and time
  • 8. Done, please deliver the trash according to the address and time of delivery.
  • 9. Monitor the progress of your order in the Order feature to track service progress.
Trash Pick Up
To access this service, you need to login/ register first. Make sure you have a Daurin account before requesting pick-up or other recycling services. Here are the steps:
  • 1. Before making a request for garbage pickup, make sure you have prepared the trash that will be sold, the trash must be of a separate type. Then pack in plastic or other containers.
  • 2. Open the Daurin application, select Start Recycling
  • 3. Next, choose the type of waste that you will sell.
  • 4. Set the estimated weight of your recycleables.
  • 5. Select the Pick Up service
  • 6. Specify the pick-up address
  • 7. Select the pick-up date and time
  • 8. Done, the collector will pick up according to the address and pick-up time.
  • 9. Monitor the progress of your order in the Order feature.

F.A.Q

What is Daurin?
Daurin is an application for buying and selling waste online (recycling platforms). Daurin connect the waste generator (house households, businesses and offices) with local collectors(collectors) closest, so easier in selling trash. Daurin focuses on patterns waste recycling in the upstream sector, not a service waste transportation or recycling handicrafts.
What is the difference between Daurin to other waste management services?
Daurin service is free used by anyone, we are not a garbage pickup service company, we try to provide more value than that, namely the waste that is picked up by our partners, all of it will be recycled and not thrown into the TPA. Whether its a service selling trash or a recycling program, everything will be recycled again.
How to sell trash through Daurin?
Selling waste through Daurin is very easy, make sure you have downloaded the Daurin application, open the Daurin application, select the "Start Recycling" feature, enter waste information, address and pick-up time, submit and wait for collectors to pick up and pay for your waste. For details, see the guide to selling trash.
What types of waste are managed by Daurin?
The waste managed by Daurin is a type of dry waste such as:
  • 1. Trash made of plastic
  • 2. Paper-based waste
  • 3. Aluminum bins and cans
  • 4. Iron and metal waste
  • 5. Electronic wastec
  • 6. Garbage glass bottles
  • 7. Donated items such as clothes and toys
  • 8. Some special waste such as used oil, glass shards, etc. (check the guide)
Is Daurin a foundation, a non-governmental organization (GO), or Community Organization?
Daurin is a social-based Technology Company. Why company? We want Daurin to be able to run independently, sustainably and have a broad impact, not relying on donations and other parties. Thus, Daurin can be sustainable and have broad environmental and social impacts.
